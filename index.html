50 Youth from each node of Navi Mumbai would be trained to learn ABCs of First-Aid, Effective CPR, & Emergency Response techniques & be a part of First-Responder 'Youth Brigade' in that node. 'Youth Brigade' shall attend to casualties during the Golden Minutes of an incident. Women & children are major casualties in any disaster; women would be trained in ERT that would help them save their kith/kin too. Emergency Response training at an young age would be an Asset, for life-long, to save lives
